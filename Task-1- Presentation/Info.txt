For this task, I designed a digital infographic using Canva to highlight key aspects of digital literacy, including safe internet practices, useful digital tools, and responsible online behavior. The aim was to present important information in a visually clear and engaging manner so that it can be easily understood by students. While creating the infographic, I focused on simplicity, proper organization, and effective use of icons and colors. This task helped me understand how to communicate complex ideas in a concise and visually appealing format, which is an important skill in the digital world.
